About Innovate Nepal

Innovate Nepal is an organization focused on fostering innovation and development within Nepal. It encompasses efforts aimed at entrepreneurship, technological advancement, and economic growth in the country. This includes supporting startups, establishing technology hubs, implementing policies to drive innovation, and investing in projects for social impact through technology.

What is HRMS?
